# Character Concept: The Eldritch Artillery

**Class:** Warlock
**Pact:** Pact of the Chain (for the Imp familiar)
**Patron:** The Fiend (often synergizes well with an Imp and aggressive casting, but we can adjust to Hexblade if you want more armor/melee options, or Genie for utility).
**Build Focus:** "Eldritch Cannon" (Maximizing Eldritch Blast damage and utility).

## Race Options for Charisma & Extra Spells
To maximize Charisma and gain an extra spell/cantrip, here are the top choices:

1. **Tiefling (Classic or Variant):** +2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence. Gives *Thaumaturgy*, *Hellish Rebuke*, and *Darkness* as racial spells. Fits perfectly with the Imp/Fiend theme.
2. **Half-Elf:** +2 Charisma, and +1 to two other stats (Dexterity and Constitution are best here). Gives two extra skill proficiencies. Excellent for a well-rounded blaster.
3. **Custom Lineage / Variant Human:** +2 to Charisma (Custom) or +1 to two stats (Human). The huge draw here is a **Free Feat at Level 1**. You could take *Fey Touched* or *Shadow Touched* (which gives +1 Charisma and extra spells like *Misty Step* or *Invisibility*) or *Spell Sniper* (to double Eldritch Blast range and ignore cover).
4. **Yuan-ti Pureblood:** +2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence. Gives *Poison Spray*, *Animal Friendship* (snakes only), and *Suggestion*. Also grants Magic Resistance (advantage on saves against spells), which is incredibly strong.
5. **Aasimar (Scourge or Protector):** +2 Charisma. Gives *Light* cantrip and a daily transformation that deals extra damage or grants flight. A fun contrast to the Imp (a celestial making a dark pact).
