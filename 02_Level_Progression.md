# Character Progression: The Eldritch Artillery

**Race:** Custom Lineage (Darkvision, +2 Cha, Free Feat)
**Class:** Warlock (The Fiend)
**Background:** Urchin (Proficiency: Sleight of Hand, Stealth, Disguise Kit, Thieves' Tools)

**Starting Stats (Level 1):**
*   **STR:** 8 (-1) - You were a starved child; you aren't lifting boulders.
*   **DEX:** 14 (+2) - Quick enough to dodge and hide.
*   **CON:** 13 (+1) - Tough enough to survive your captor.
*   **INT:** 10 (+0) - Average street smarts.
*   **WIS:** 12 (+1) - Above-average awareness, honed on the streets.
*   **CHA:** 18 (+4) - 15 base + 2 (Racial) + 1 (Fey Touched Feat). The sheer force of your will and the infernal contract.

---

## Level 1: The Survivor Awakens
*   **Hit Points:** 9 (8 + 1 Con)
*   **Proficiencies:** Light Armor, Simple Weapons. Skills: Deception, Intimidation.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Dark One's Blessing (Fiend Patron):** When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 HP, you gain Temporary HP equal to your Charisma modifier + Warlock Level (currently 5 THP). This makes you surprisingly tanky when you get kills.
*   **Feat (Custom Lineage): Fey Touched**
    *   +1 to Charisma (bringing it to 18).
    *   You learn the *Misty Step* spell and one 1st-level spell of your choice (recommendation: **Hex** or *Silvery Barbs* if allowed). You can cast each of these for free once per long rest, or using your Warlock spell slots.
*   **Spells Known:**
    *   Cantrips: **Eldritch Blast** (Your main weapon), *Minor Illusion* (For trickery and hiding).
    *   1st Level: *Armor of Agathys* (More THP and damage to attackers), *Burning Hands* (Fiend spell for AoE).

## Level 2: The Eldritch Cannon is Loaded
*   **Hit Points:** 17 (10 + 5 + 2)
*   **Features:**
    *   **Eldritch Invocations (Pick 2):**
        1.  **Agonizing Blast:** Add your Charisma modifier to the damage of every Eldritch Blast beam. (Essential).
        2.  **Repelling Blast:** When you hit a creature with Eldritch Blast, you can push it 10 feet away. (Crucial for keeping enemies off you and pushing them off cliffs or into hazards).
*   **Spells Known:** Add *Command* (Fiend spell) or *Hellish Rebuke*.

## Level 3: Xil Manifests
*   **Hit Points:** 24
*   **Features:**
    *   **Pact Boon: Pact of the Chain.** You learn the *Find Familiar* spell and can cast it as a ritual. Xil, who has been watching from the shadows, is finally able to cross the veil and manifest physically. You choose an **Imp** form for him. Xil can turn invisible, fly, and attack using your reaction. He is the ultimate scout and your patron's fully-realized watcher.
*   **Spells Known:** Swap out a 1st level spell for a 2nd level spell. Pick up *Scorching Ray* or *Shatter* for more blast power. You also gain 2nd-level spell slots (which refresh on a short rest).

## Level 4: Sharpening the Will
*   **Hit Points:** 31
*   **Features:**
    *   **Ability Score Improvement:** Your Charisma is already 18. You can take +2 Charisma to max it out at 20, or take a powerful feat.
        *   Recommendation: **+2 Charisma** (Boosts your attack, damage, spell DC, and Temporary HP all at once). Alternatively, **War Caster** if you are struggling with Concentration checks.
*   **Spells Known:** Add another cantrip (e.g., *Mage Hand* or *Prestidigitation*) and a 2nd-level spell like *Misty Step* (if you didn't take it earlier) or *Suggestion*.

## Level 5: The Artillery Battery
*   **Hit Points:** 38
*   **Features:**
    *   **Eldritch Blast Upgrade:** Your cantrip now fires **two beams**. This means two attacks, two chances to apply Agonizing Blast damage, and two chances to push 10 feet with Repelling Blast.
    *   **Eldritch Invocation (Pick 1):** Pick **Investment of the Chain Master**. This makes Xil significantly stronger. He gains a flying speed, you can command him to attack as a bonus action, his attacks bypass resistances, and enemies must use your Spell Save DC against his poison sting (which is lethal at this level).
*   **Spells Known:** 3rd-level spell slots! Pick up **Fireball** (Fiend spell) to clear rooms.

---
*(This progression takes you through the core build. From Level 6 onward, you continue to scale your Charisma to 20, grab more invocations for utility, and hurl increasingly devastating Fiend spells like Wall of Fire.)*
