# Brenya's Grimoire: Level 1 Spells

This tome contains the complete list of 1st-level spells available to Brenya through her Warlock class and The Fiend patron. 

*(Note: In the 2024 rules, you can swap your "Chosen" spells for any of these at the end of a Long Rest. In 2014, you can only swap one spell when you level up).*

---

## 😈 Fiend Patron Spells (Always Prepared in 2024)
These spells are granted by your infernal contract.
*   **Burning Hands:** (Action). 15-foot cone. Creatures must make a DC 14 Dex save or take 3d6 Fire damage (half on success). Flammable objects catch fire.
*   **Command:** (Action). Target must succeed on a DC 14 Wis save or follow a one-word command (e.g., "Halt," "Drop," "Flee") on its next turn. Undead and creatures that don't understand you are immune.

---

## 📖 Warlock 1st-Level Spells (Available to Choose)

### Currently Chosen / Granted via Feat:
*   **Armor of Agathys:** (Action). Gain 5 THP. While you have these THP, any creature that hits you with a melee attack takes 5 Cold damage. Lasts 1 hour.
*   **Hellish Rebuke:** (Reaction). When damaged by a creature within 60 feet, force a DC 14 Dex save. The creature takes 2d10 Fire damage (half on success).
*   **Hex:** (Bonus Action). *Concentration.* Target takes an extra 1d6 necrotic damage when you hit it with an attack. Target has disadvantage on ability checks of one chosen stat.

### Unchosen Spells (Your Options):
*   **Arms of Hadar:** (Action). Tendrils of dark energy erupt from you. All creatures within 10 feet must make a Str save or take 2d6 necrotic damage and lose their reaction until their next turn (half damage on success).
*   **Charm Person:** (Action). Humanoid target must make a Wis save (with advantage if you are fighting it). On a failure, it is Charmed by you for 1 hour and treats you as a friendly acquaintance.
*   **Comprehend Languages:** (Action / Ritual). For 1 hour, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language you hear, and any written language you see (must touch the surface).
*   **Expeditious Retreat:** (Bonus Action). *Concentration.* For 10 minutes, you can take the Dash action as a Bonus Action on each of your turns. Great for extreme mobility.
*   **Illusory Script:** (1 Minute / Ritual). Write a message that appears to be gibberish or a different message to anyone except the creatures you designate. Lasts 10 days.
*   **Protection from Evil and Good:** (Action). *Concentration.* For 10 minutes, aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead have Disadvantage on attack rolls against the target. The target also can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them.
*   **Unseen Servant:** (Action / Ritual). Creates an invisible, mindless, shapeless force that performs simple tasks at your command for 1 hour (fetching things, cleaning, opening doors).
*   **Witch Bolt:** (Action). *Concentration.* Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, deal 1d12 lightning damage. On each of your subsequent turns, you can use your Action to automatically deal 1d12 lightning damage to the target. Ends if the target moves out of range (30ft) or behind total cover.
