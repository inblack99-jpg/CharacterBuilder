## Background: Urchin (The Abandoned Captive)
You were an abandoned child, a nobody left to rot in the cruelest corners of the world. Worse still, you were taken by an abusive captor—someone who delighted in breaking those smaller and weaker than themselves. You didn't survive by being good, or by waiting for a hero. You survived because in your darkest, most desperate moment, you called out into the void... and something terrible answered.

You traded your soul for the power to burn your cage down. The magic you wield isn't a gift; it is a weapon forged from your own desperation. 

You hate evil. You hate those who prey on the weak. But you know that stopping them requires a willingness to be as ruthless as they are. You are the monster that hunts other monsters, armed with hellfire and an Eldritch Blast that hits like a siege weapon.

### The Infernal Contract
**The Patron:** The Fiend
**The Enforcer:** Xil (The Imp)

The agreement was not written on parchment. It was seared into your soul in a moment of pure survival instinct. 

**Terms of the Pact:**
1. **The Gift:** The Fiend grants you the arcane firepower necessary to ensure you are never helpless again.
2. **The Price:** Upon your ultimate demise, your soul is forfeit to the infernal planes, to serve your Patron for eternity.
3. **The Watcher:** You are bound to a familiar, an Imp named **Xil**. Xil is your scout, your spy, and occasionally your combat assistant. However, Xil's primary purpose is to act as the Patron's eyes and ears. He is the enforcer of the contract. If you attempt to seek redemption, break the pact, or act against the Patron's interests, Xil is there to remind you of the consequences—sometimes with venom, sometimes with whispers.

Xil is both your closest ally and your ever-present jailer. Your dynamic is one of mutual usefulness layered over deep mistrust.
