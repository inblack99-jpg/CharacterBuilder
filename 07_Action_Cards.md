# 🃏 Combat Action Cards (Level 1 Warlock - 2024 Rules)

Use this quick reference to build your turns during combat. You get **1 Action**, **1 Bonus Action**, and **1 Reaction** per round.

---

## 🟢 YOUR ACTIONS (Pick 1 per turn)

### 🪄 Eldritch Blast (Cantrip)
*   **Time:** 1 Action
*   **Range:** 120 ft.
*   **Attack Roll:** 1d20 + 6
*   **Damage:** 1d10 + 4 Force
*   **Note:** If the target is Hexed, add 1d6 Necrotic damage.

### 🪄 Friends (Cantrip)
*   **Time:** 1 Action
*   **Range:** 10 ft. (Non-Combat typically)
*   **Effect:** You have Advantage on all Charisma checks directed at one non-hostile creature of your choice. Lasts 1 minute.

### 🪄 Burning Hands (1 Spell Slot)
*   **Time:** 1 Action
*   **Range:** 15-foot cone extending from you.
*   **Effect:** All creatures in the cone make a DC 14 Dex Save.
*   **Damage:** 3d6 Fire (half on a successful save).

### 🪄 Command (1 Spell Slot)
*   **Time:** 1 Action
*   **Range:** 60 ft.
*   **Effect:** Target makes a DC 14 Wis Save. If they fail, they must follow a single one-word command (e.g., "Drop," "Halt," "Flee") on their next turn.

### 🛡️ Armor of Agathys (1 Spell Slot)
*   **Time:** 1 Action
*   **Duration:** 1 Hour
*   **Effect:** You gain **5 Temporary Hit Points**. While you have these Temp HP, any creature that hits you with a melee attack automatically takes **5 Cold Damage**.

---

## 🟡 YOUR BONUS ACTIONS (Pick 1 per turn)

### 🪄 Hex (Free Cast or 1 Spell Slot)
*   **Time:** 1 Bonus Action
*   **Range:** 90 ft.
*   **Duration:** 1 Hour (Requires Concentration).
*   **Effect:** Target takes an extra **1d6 Necrotic damage** every time YOU hit it with an attack. Target has Disadvantage on ability checks made with one stat of your choice (e.g., Strength, to make them easy to grapple).
*   **Re-cast:** If the target dies, use a Bonus Action on a later turn to move the Hex to a new target.

### 💨 Misty Step (Free Cast or 1 Spell Slot)
*   **Time:** 1 Bonus Action
*   **Range:** 30 ft.
*   **Effect:** You teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Great for escaping melee combat without taking an opportunity attack.

### 😈 Command Xil to Attack (Pact of the Chain)
*   **Time:** 1 Bonus Action
*   **Requirement:** Xil must be summoned, in Imp form, and within 100 feet of you.
*   **Effect:** Xil takes the Attack action on his turn.

---

## 🔴 YOUR REACTIONS (Pick 1 per round)

### 🔥 Hellish Rebuke (1 Spell Slot)
*   **Trigger:** You take damage from a creature you can see within 60 feet.
*   **Time:** 1 Reaction
*   **Effect:** The attacker makes a DC 14 Dex Save.
*   **Damage:** 2d10 Fire damage (half on a successful save).

### 🗡️ Opportunity Attack (Mundane)
*   **Trigger:** A hostile creature moves out of your melee reach (5 ft).
*   **Time:** 1 Reaction
*   **Attack Roll (Dagger):** 1d20 + 4
*   **Damage (Dagger):** 1d4 + 2 Piercing

---

## 🦇 XIL'S ACTIONS (Imp Familiar)

Xil rolls his own Initiative and takes his own turn.

### 🦂 Sting Attack (Requires Your Bonus Action to Command)
*   **Time:** 1 Action (Xil's)
*   **Attack Roll:** 1d20 + 5 (typically, or your spell attack modifier depending on DM ruling for 2024 familiars).
*   **Damage:** 1d8 + 4 (Does NOT trigger your Hex).

### 👻 Invisibility (Free for Xil)
*   **Time:** 1 Action (Xil's)
*   **Effect:** Xil magically turns Invisible until he attacks or until his concentration ends.

### 🤝 The Help Action (Free for Xil)
*   **Time:** 1 Action (Xil's)
*   **Effect:** Xil feints or distracts a target within 5 feet of him. The next attack roll made against that target (like your Eldritch Blast) has **Advantage**.
*   **Note:** Doing this does NOT break his Invisibility.

### 🦅 Shapechanger (Free for Xil)
*   **Time:** 1 Action (Xil's)
*   **Effect:** Xil polymorphs into a **Raven** (50ft fly), a **Rat** (20ft walk), or a **Spider** (20ft climb). He can still turn invisible in these forms, but he cannot attack.
