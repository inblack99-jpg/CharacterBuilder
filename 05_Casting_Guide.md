# Brenya's Eldritch Casting Guide (Level 1)

This guide summarizes your tactical options for using your **1 Warlock Spell Slot** and your **Fey Touched Free Spells** effectively in combat.

---

## 1. Your Magical "Currency"
At Level 1, you have three distinct "charges" of magic per day:
*   **1 Warlock Spell Slot:** Recharges on a **Short Rest** (1 hour) or via **Magical Cunning** (1 minute, 2024 rules). Can be used for *any* 1st-level spell you know.
*   **1 Free Cast of *Hex*:** Recharges on a **Long Rest**.
*   **1 Free Cast of *Misty Step*:** Recharges on a **Long Rest**.

---

## 2. Spell Categorization

### **Maximum Damage (The Nukes)**
*   **Burning Hands (1 Warlock Slot):** 3d6 Fire damage in a 15ft cone. Best used when 2+ enemies are clumped together. (DC 14 Dex save for half).
*   **Hex (Free Fey Touched Cast):** Adds +1d6 Necrotic damage *every time* you hit with an attack. Best for single-target boss fights.

### **Protective & Defensive (The Shields)**
*   **Armor of Agathys (1 Warlock Slot):** Gain 5 Temp HP. Anyone who hits you in melee takes **5 Cold damage** automatically. Lasts 1 hour or until Temp HP is gone.
*   **Dark One's Blessing (Passive):** Gain **5 Temp HP** automatically whenever you reduce a hostile creature to 0 HP. (No action or slot required!)
*   **Misty Step (Free Fey Touched Cast):** Bonus Action teleport (30 ft). Perfect for escaping a grapple or moving away from a melee brute without taking an Opportunity Attack.

**NOTE ON DEFENSE:** Temporary Hit Points **DO NOT STACK**. If you have 5 THP from *Armor of Agathys* and kill an enemy (triggering 5 THP from *Dark One's Blessing*), you must choose which 5 to keep. *Always keep the ones from Armor of Agathys to maintain the reflective cold damage!*

### **Situational & Reactionary (The Punishers)**
*   **Hellish Rebuke (1 Warlock Slot):** Reaction cast when you take damage. Deals 2d10 Fire damage to the attacker. (DC 14 Dex save for half).
*   **Command (1 Warlock Slot):** One-word command (e.g., "Grovel," "Drop"). Target must succeed on a DC 14 Wis save or lose their next turn following the command.

---

## 3. Battle Endurance: What "Stays Up"?

If you move from Battle 1 to Battle 2 with **no rest**, use this logic:

| Spell | Stays Up? | How to maintain / use in next battle |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Hex** | **YES** | Maintain **Concentration**. Use a **Bonus Action** in the next battle to move it to a new target. |
| **Armor of Agathys** | **YES** | Lasts 1 hour. If you still have at least 1 Temp HP from the spell, it's still active. |
| **Burning Hands** | **NO** | Instantaneous. The fire is gone; you must cast it again. |
| **Hellish Rebuke** | **NO** | Instantaneous reaction. Must be cast again. |
| **Misty Step** | **NO** | Instantaneous movement. Must be cast again. |

---

## 4. Standard Tactical Strategy (The "Brenya Loop")

1.  **Preparation (Optional):** If you know an ambush is coming, cast **Armor of Agathys** *before* the door opens to walk in with 5 THP and reflective damage.
2.  **Turn 1:** Use **Bonus Action** to cast **Hex** (Free Cast) on the biggest threat.
3.  **Action:** Cast **Eldritch Blast**. (Damage: 1d10 + 4 Force + 1d6 Necrotic).
4.  **The Reserve:** Save your **1 Warlock Slot** for a mid-battle emergency:
    *   *Burning Hands* if you get swarmed.
    *   *Hellish Rebuke* if a squishy enemy hits you.
    *   *Armor of Agathys* if your current shields are down and you are surrounded.
    *   *Command* if a dangerous enemy is trying to flee or reach an ally.

---

## 5. Long-Range Strategy (The "Sniper" Approach)

Eldritch Blast has a range of **120 feet**, while *Hex* only has a range of **90 feet**. This creates a "Dead Zone" between 120ft and 90ft.

### **The Invisible Scout (Pre-Combat)**
*   **Xil's Role:** Send Xil (Imp) ahead of you while **Invisible**. He has 40ft fly speed and can hover right over targets (within 5ft) without being seen or heard.

### **Tactical Execution**

| Distance | Your Action | Your Bonus Action | Xil's Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **120+ ft** | Eldritch Blast (Action) | Command **Xil** to Attack | Xil "Alpha Strikes" from Invisibility. |
| **90 ft** | Eldritch Blast (Action) | Cast **Hex** (Bonus Action) | Xil continues to harass/distract targets. |

**The Strategy:**
1.  **Turn 1 (120ft):** Fire your Blast (Action) and command Xil to sting (Bonus Action). **Move 30 feet forward.**
2.  **Turn 2 (90ft):** Now that you are in range, cast **Hex** (Bonus Action) and Blast again (Action). You now have the full curse + blast combo running.
