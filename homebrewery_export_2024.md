## Background: The Escaped Captive (2024)
In the 2024 rules, Brenya's background provides her core Ability Score increases (+2 Cha, +1 Dex) and her initial feats.

### The Manifestation of Xil
Unlike the older rules, the 2024 Warlock is able to draw upon their Pact Boon immediately. Xil has been with Brenya since the day she escaped her captor, acting not just as a watcher, but as a physical shield. 

The relationship is tense; Xil is a direct manifestation of the debt she owes, yet he is the only constant in her life since she was abandoned.
